Let Me Do It For You

A doggy platformer with lengthening nose🐕

Nov 23, 2024
1 minute read

Introduction

Let Me Do It For You is a weird platformer about a dog who can extend his nose and, not only the nose can stretch GREAT GREAT GREAT distance, it can also change directions lmao.

More Infos:

  • Link to itch.io: https://zeenaz.itch.io/let-me-do-it-for-you

Personal Thoughts

Memes

From the title one can tell that this game is inspired from the Borzoi Dog meme. Personally I have nothing much to say about this theme, it is cute and really, really funny.

Growth

With the former experiences in mind, I am able to finished my tasks quicker and smoother this time. I also managed to study new components of Unity when facing unfamiliar problems. When creating the nose stretching effects, I researched and find a Unity component called Line Renderer that can draw textured lines. Using scripts to control this component, the body of the nose is drawn as the nose head moves, creating the effect of nose stretching.

Important Lessons

  • Always make backup files before making CRAZY changes to the codes
  • Some mechanics are just complex and unnecessary
  • Borzoi Dogs are funny
